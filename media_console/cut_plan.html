Media Console – Plywood Cut Plan

Material: 3/4" plywood  ·  1 sheet 4×8 (48" × 96")

Sheet 1 of 1  ·  48" × 96"

Scale: 6 px = 1"  ·  Dashed red lines = saw cuts
Add 1/8" kerf allowance per cut  ·  Grain runs along 96" axis (vertical)

Cut List — 7 pieces from 1 sheet

Label Finished Size Qty
A, B 55½" × 15" 2
C 55½" × 4" 1
D, E 16¼" × 15" 2
F, G 14¾" × 14¼" 2

Sheet utilization: ~61%  ·  Offcut: ~14"×96" strip + scraps

Cut Sequence

  1. Rip full sheet at 34" from left edge (full 96" length)
    → 34"×96" working panel  +  14"×96" offcut strip (set aside)
  2. Cross-cut the 34"×96" panel at 55½"
    → Top: 34"×55½"  ·  Bottom: 34"×40½"
  3. From top section (34"×55½"):
    Rip at 15" → A (15"×55½")
    Rip at 15" → B (15"×55½")
    Remaining 4"×55½" → C
  4. From bottom section (34"×40½"):
    Rip at 16¼" → first 16¼"×40½" strip
    Rip at 16¼" → second 16¼"×40½" strip
    (1½" remainder is scrap)
  5. From first 16¼" strip:
    Cross-cut at 15" → D
    From remainder (16¼"×25½"): cross-cut at 14¼", rip at 14¾" → F
  6. Repeat step 5 on second 16¼" strip → E and G

Tip: make all rip cuts before cross-cuts where possible to keep pieces supported on the table saw.
